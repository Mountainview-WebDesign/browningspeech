Home

Browning Speech Therapy, LLC provides customized speech therapy services in the comfort of your home. Speech therapy services are “play-based” and individualized to meet the needs of the child and their family. Comprehensive evaluations, therapy, parent training, and consultative services are available.

Serving Children Who Are Tackling…

  • Apraxia of Speech
  • Articulation Disorders/Phonological Processes
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Cleft Palate
  • Delays in Early Language Development (Late Talkers)
  • Developmental Delays
  • Down Syndrome
  • Expressive Language Delay
  • Feeding Disorders
  • Fluency Disorders (Stuttering)
  • Receptive Language Delays
  • Reading
  • Sensory Integration Disorders Related to Speech
