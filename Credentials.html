Beth Browning, MS CCC-SLP is an ASHA certified Speech Language Pathologist with over 14 years of experience.  She received both her B.S. In Communication Disorders and her M.S. in Speech Language Pathology from Utah State University.  Having  gained experience working in a variety of clinical settings across the United States, Beth has an eclectic therapy style.  She draws from years of experience working with children along with extensive continuing education and certifications.  She specializes in working with children who have Autism Spectrum Disorder, Apraxia of speech, Articulation Delays, Feeding Disorders and “Late Talkers”.
