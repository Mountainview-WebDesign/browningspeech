Horsing Around Summer Speech Program


“Horsing Around” is a unique speech therapy program offered in conjunction with RESOLUTION HORSE COMPANY.  Children are provided an opportunity to work on their speech goals while interacting with horses.  This can be a very motivating setting for children, offering a fun approach to achieving their communication goals.  Sessions are tailored to the child’s goals.  Family participation is highly encouraged.

What are the advantages to doing Speech Therapy in your home?


  1.   Children learn best in their natural environment.

  2.   Parents can be more involved in the therapy services reducing the amount of therapy needed.

  3.   No paying the high cost of gas traveling to and from speech therapy.

  4.   No long car rides taking time out of your busy daily schedule.

  5.   No need to find babysitters for siblings or sit in waiting rooms trying to entertain your other children.

  6.   Less exposure to germs.
